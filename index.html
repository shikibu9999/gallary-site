ABOUT

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch painter belonging to the post-impressionist movement,
particularly known for his pursuit of the radiance of the sun in his works.
His paintings explore the beauty of plants, trees, and landscapes,
characterized by vivid colors and expressions of sunlight.
He later became associated with the post-impressionists,
creating works influenced by symbolism such as "The Starry Night" produced in Arles, France.

MUSEUM

VanGoghMuseum

The Van Gogh Museum is an art museum located in Amsterdam, Netherlands,
which primarily houses works by the 19th-century Dutch painter Vincent van Gogh.
The museum has a collection of over 200 paintings,
including many of his most famous works created
in the Netherlands, France, and during his final days in Arles.
Visitors can learn about Van Gogh's life
and art through his extensive collection of works.
In addition to Van Gogh's paintings,
the museum also showcases works by other artists who were active during the same period.
It is a popular attraction for visitors throughout the year.
